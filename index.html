How It Works

Three steps. Zero friction.

No settings to configure, no buttons to press. Just chat like you normally do.

1

Pick your language

When you sign up, choose the language you read in. That's it. One choice.

2

Chat with anyone

Write in your language. Your message is translated instantly and invisibly for the other person.

3

Everyone reads in theirs

Each person sees every message in their own language. Like the conversation was always in one language.

Features

A real chat app. Not a translator.

Hiola works like the messaging apps you already use, but everyone reads in their language.

Age-aware translation

Messages to a 7-year-old use simple words. Messages to a grandparent feel warm and respectful. The tone adapts automatically.

10 languages

English, Hebrew, Russian, Spanish, Arabic, Chinese, French, German, Portuguese, and Ukrainian. More coming soon.

Group chats

One group, many languages. Everyone writes in theirs, everyone reads in theirs. Family group chats finally make sense.

Photo sharing

Share photos from your camera or library. Captions get translated too, so the moment is understood by everyone.
